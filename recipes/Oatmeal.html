Oatmeal

Budget and Tasty oatmeal version, ideal for hungry students!

Oatmeal full of your favourite ingredients like : apples, cinnamon and honey!

ingredients

  • 60g oats
  • An apple
  • yogurt
  • cinnamon
  • honey

Instructions

  1. Cut the apple in half and grate one half in an empty bow
  2. Add 2 table spoons of yogurt, cinnamon on taste and stir together
  3. Add the oats, mix well and let it soak for 5 to 10 minutes
  4. chop the rest of the apple on little cubes
  5. toast 20g of oats, the apple cubes in a pan, add some cinnamon on top and mix the ingredients together. Keep doing it for about a minute
  6. Spread the soaked oats in a bow, add the ingredients from the pan on top of it, add a bit of yogurt if you feel like it and a drizzle of honey
  7. Enjoy your dish :)!
