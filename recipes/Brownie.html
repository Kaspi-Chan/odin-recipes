ingredients

  • 2 chocolate bars
  • Butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tea cup of sugar
  • 1 tea cup of flour
  • A pinch of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Break the chocolates into small pieces and place them in a bowl on a water bath, along with the butter (measure on taste, the more the merrier)
  2. In another bowl beat 3 eggs and one tea cup of sugar
  3. In a third bowl add 1 tea cup of flour and a pinch of baking powder
  4. After the chocolate and the butter have melted mix them very well and add them in the egg mixture
  5. After that, gradually add the mixture of powder, while stiring everything
  6. Place a baking papper on a tray and add the mixtured
  7. Bake for 20 minutes on 180 degrees on upper and lower mode(you can check the brownies with a toothpick)
